6th Chrysanthemum Cup: The Yearly two-day open tournament of JMSA Atsugi

Abstract

Japan Mahjong Sports Association (JMSA) Atsugi branch will host its 6th two-day open torunament Chrysanthemum Cup (Cho-yo hai) in September 2016.

Date and time

September 17 and 18, 2016: Start on Saturday afternoon and finish in Sunday evening.

Venue

Atsugi Kenko Mahjong Center Yotsuba-kai: Two-minute walk from Hon-atsugi station.

Fee

JPY 10,000 (including the dinner of the first day and the lunch for the second day).

Rules

Official rules of Japan Mahjong Sports Association (Seven pairs and All types cannot combined, limit of 88 points, etc).

Registration

For players from abroad, contact me (Ryuichi OHORI) via Facebook or by email for registration or inquiry. The maximum number of players is 56.

Venue

Atsugi Kenko Mahjong Center Yotsuba-kai: Naka-cho 3-1-17, Atsugi-shi, Kanagawa-ken. Tel: +81-462259566 (available only on the days of tournament)

The nearest station is Hon-atsugi of Odakyu Line: 50-minute ride from Shinjuku. It is convenient to get on a bus if you arrive at Haneda International Airport.

Structure

  • Mainly pairs, and individual competition
  • The score of a pair is the sum of two players' table points [4, 2, 1, 0].
  • The score of a individual player is the sum of the mini-points.
  • The table assignment of Session 6 is determined according to individual ranking, but without playing against your partner
  • Prizes are for (this is the last year's: this is subject to change):
    • Top 3 pairs
    • Lucky 7th pair
    • Middle-ranked pair
    • Top 3 individuals
    • Lucky 7th individual
    • Hassaku (a Japanese citrus hybrid) (8(HAchi)th and 39(SAN-ju-KU)th) individuals
    • Middle-ranked individual
    • 5, 10, 15, ... th individuals
    • O-nami (Big wave): the pair with biggest individual score difference
    • Ko-nami (small wave): the pair with smallest individual score difference
    • Session best scores
    • Session worst scores
